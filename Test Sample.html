    Knipper announces its new affiliate KnippeRx, Specialty Pharmacy
    Charlestown, IN. October 19, 2017 – KnippeRx Inc. announces the official opening of their specialty pharmacy services company providing focused hub services, patient dispensing, as well as integrated distribution through its affiliate J. Knipper and Company, Inc.

    Partners

    Veeva Systems, well known as a leader in CRM, Content Management, Customer Master Solutions, and Customer and Key Stakeholder Data Solutions, is a valued partner of J. Knipper and Company. Knipper’s sample accountability and direct to practitioner fulfillment services have been integrated with Veeva to offer seamless data transfers of the sampling activity captured in the Veeva CRM.

    Knipper and WarehouseAnywhere have combined their knowledge and expertise in the pharmaceutical and storage industries to provide customers a single point of contact for sample accountability, field audits and closeouts, and storage unit procurement and management.

    Together with Genesis Logistics, Knipper provides specialized “white glove” distribution services for product and literature distribution to Pharmaceutical, Biotech, and Medical Device field sales forces which include pre-scheduling and delivery windows as narrow as 1 hour.
